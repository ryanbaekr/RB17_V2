# RB17_V2
 
a fixed layout sandwich numpad with backlighting

# Case

switch should be cut in a 1.5mm material

bottom can be cut in a 1.5mm or 3.0mm material

6 m3 15mm standoffs and 12 m3 8mm bolts are needed for assembly

# PCB

schematic and gerber files are available

1 ardiuno pro micro/qwiic pro micro/elite-c, 2 12 pin male headers, (optional) 1 3 pin angled male header, (optional) 1 jumper, 17 cherry compatible switches, 17 1n4148 diodes, (optional) 17 leds, (optional) 17 resistors, 3 2u costar/cherry plate mount stabilizers, and keycaps are needed for assembly

# Firmware

firmware is maintained in the QMK repository
